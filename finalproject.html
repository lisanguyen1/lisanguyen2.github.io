Lisa's Javascript Programs

This program is a version of the game "Stack" by Ketchlapp that Alexis and I programmed for CSC 106's final project. Your goal is to stack 20 blocks on top of each other accurately. If you're able to get to the end you win and if you ran out of blocks, it's game over. More layers will float in off of the left or right side of the screen. When the block floats over the stack, mouse click and ti will drop, adding another level to the tower. The user can also customize their experience by picking which theme they want to play the game in. There are 6 options to choose from.
